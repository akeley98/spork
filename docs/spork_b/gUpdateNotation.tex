\magicSubsection{Update Notation}{sec:gUpdateNotation}

$\rho[a_0 \to b_0, a_1 \to b_1, ...]$ means the function $\rho$ with outputs for $a_0, a_1,...$ modified:
\begin{equation*}
    \rho[...](x) = \begin{cases}
        b_0 & \text{if } x = a_0 \\
        b_1 & \text{if } x = a_1 \\
        ... & ... \\
        \rho(x) & \text{if no other case}
    \end{cases}
\end{equation*}
